My most impressive powers

Super Healing

Super healing has been one my many strengths. After the battle, I have gash on my arm which quickly healed with no sign it had ever been there in the first place.

Telepathy

My powers of telepathy are not always on full display and when they have been, the Amazon has used it sparingly.

Flight

Sometimes, navigating about through the skies in an invisible jet is not all it’s cracked up to be. I am able to fly around with the same near-light speed as the quick messenger god.

Super speed

There’s no doubt about it, I am fast. In the blink of an eye, I can be in one place and end up in another.I can most certainly catch my foes off-guard with how speedy I can be.

Super strength

I have superhuman strength, meaning I can lift things like a tank with little to no effort at all. When my gauntlets are removed, my power becomes virtually limitless.

Magical powers

During an encounter with a villain by the name of Upside-Down Man, he senses my latent magical powers and unlocks them out of curiosity. My power was something that not even Zatanna had ever encountered, and is definitely a game changer for the Amazon.
