Skills

My most impressive powers

Healing

Super healing has been one my many strengths. After the battle, I have gash on my arm which quickly healed with no sign it had ever been there in the first place.

Telepathy

My powers of telepathy are not always on full display and when they have been, the Amazon has used it sparingly.

Speed

There’s no doubt about it, I am fast. In the blink of an eye, I can be in one place and end up in another.I can most certainly catch my foes off-guard with how speedy I can be.

Strength

I have superhuman strength, meaning I can lift things like a tank with little to no effort at all. When my gauntlets are removed, my power becomes virtually limitless.

Flight

Sometimes, navigating about through the skies in an invisible jet is not all it’s cracked up to be. I am able to fly around with the same near-light speed as the quick messenger god.

Magical powers

During an encounter with a villain by the name of Upside-Down Man, he senses my latent magical powers and unlocks them out of curiosity. My power was something that no one encountered it, and is definitely a game changer for the Amazon.

Assets

I may believe in peace. but I'm always prepared for war. In my fight to defend the innocent people of Earth, I arms myself with the tools I need : The weapons of god.

Lasso

Hephaestus, god of smiths, forged super-strong lasso. It can force whoever is caught in it to tell the truth.

Bow and Arrow

I rarely use modern, human weapons. I prefer the traditional weapons that I trained with, such as bow and arrow.

Sword

Other than my lasso the sowrd is my favourite weapon. Perfectly balanced and stronger than steel. I can cut through almost anything.

Shield

My shield was created by the Amazons. I carry it into battle when people need more protection than I my bracelets can safely offer

Tiara

My tiara does more than show my princess statut. It is a razor-sharp weapon that will always return to her hand w hen she throws it.

Bracelets

The amazons wear bracelets as a reminder to never let anyone controle them. My special bracelets are unbreakable, can block bullets and are also able to deflect lasers.
